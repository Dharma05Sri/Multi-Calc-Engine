System Protocols (FAQ)

How does the Safe-Bunk algorithm calculate attendance?
The matrix mathematically projects your total conducted classes against your current presence. It isolates the exact numerical margin you have before your ratio drops below the mandatory 75% baseline.
Is the Wealth Projector (SIP) guaranteed?
The Wealth Projector utilizes strict mathematical compounding based on the Expected Return percentage you input. It does not account for real-world market volatility, inflation adjustments, or taxation.
Is my financial and biometric data stored on your servers?
No. NexusCalc is a client-side architecture. All calculations (SIP, EMI, BMI) are executed entirely within your local browser. Zero personal data is transmitted or stored on AstraForge databases.
How does the AI Command Terminal work?
The terminal utilizes a lightweight Natural Language Processing (NLP) regex script. You can type human-readable queries like "15% of 85000" and the engine will instantly extract and compute the numeric variables.
